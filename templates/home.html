The Severance Procedure

The cornerstone of our philosophy is the Severance Procedure, a safe, simple, and reversible medical procedure that bifurcates your memory. When you are at work, you will have no recollection of your personal life, and when you leave, you will have no memory of your work. This creates two distinct, purposeful individuals: your "innie," dedicated to the important work we do at Lumon, and your "outie," free to live life to its fullest.
